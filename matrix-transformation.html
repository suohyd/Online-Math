Example 4.2.2. (Linear transformation given by a matrix multiplication).
Let \(F\) be a field and consider an \(n\)-dimensional vector space \(F^n\text{.}\) Suppose \(A\in M_n(F)\text{.}\) The map
\begin{equation*} T_A\colon F^n\to F^n\quad\text{given by}\quad x\mapsto Ax^t \end{equation*}
is an \(F\)-linear transformation.
