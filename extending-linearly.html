Remark 4.1.5. (Linear extension of a map).
A linear transformation on a vector space is completely determined by its action on a basis. Indeed, if \(\{v_i\}\) is a basis of \(V\) and \(v\in V\) then \(v=\sum\alpha_iv_i\text{.}\) Using properties of linear transformation we have \(T(v)=\sum\alpha_iT(v_i)\text{.}\) This process is called extending map linearly on \(V\) or linear extension of the map.
