Theorem 3.4.7.
Let \(V\) be a finite-dimensional vector space over \(F\text{,}\) and let \(\{e_1,e_2,\ldots,e_n\}\) be a basis of \(V\text{.}\) If \(\mathfrak{B}=\{f_1,f_2,\ldots,f_r\}\subseteq V\) is linearly independent then, \(\mathfrak{B}\) can be extended to a basis of \(V\) by adding \(n-r\) vectors from \(\{e_1,e_2,\ldots,e_n\}\text{.}\)
