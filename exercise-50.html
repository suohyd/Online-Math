Exercise 2.
Let \(V=M_{1\times n}(F)\) and \(W=M_{n\times 1}(F)\) be vector spaces over a field \(F\text{.}\) Let \(e_i=(\delta_{i1},\delta_{i2},\ldots,\delta_{in})\text{.}\) Consider ordered bases \(\mathfrak{B}_V=\{e_i:1\leq i\leq n\}\) and \(\mathfrak{B}_W=(e_i^t:1\leq i\leq n)\) of \(V\) and \(W\text{,}\) respectively. Consider the linear transformation \(T\colon V\to W\) given by \(x\mapsto x^t\text{.}\)
