Lemma 3.4.2.
Let \(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_n\in V\) (\(n>1\)) and let \(v_i^\prime=v_i\) for \(i=1,2,\ldots,n-1\) and \(v_n^\prime=v_n+\alpha v_1\) for some \(\alpha\in F\text{.}\) Then, \(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_n\) are linearly independent if and only if \(v_1^\prime,v_2^\prime,\ldots,v_n^\prime\) are linearly independent.
