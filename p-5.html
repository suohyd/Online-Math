Let us denote by

  1. \(T_{pq}(\alpha)\) the matrix obtained by multiplying \(q\)-th row of \(I_n\) by \(\alpha\in F\) and adding this row to \(p\)-th row of \(I_n\text{;}\)

  2. \(D_p(\beta)\) the matrix obtained by multiplying \(p\)-th row of \(I_n\) by \(\beta\in F\setminus\{0\}\text{;}\)

  3. \(P_{rs}\) be the matrix obtained by interchanging \(r\)-th row with \(s\)-th row of \(I_n\text{.}\)
