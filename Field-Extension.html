Section 1.2

We now state a special property of ring homomorphism from a field.

Lemma 1.2.1.
Let \(F\) be a field and let \(A\) be a nonzero ring with unity. Any ring homomorphism \(f\colon F\to A\) is injective.
Proof.
Hint: Note that \(\ker\,f\) is an ideal of \(F\text{.}\)
